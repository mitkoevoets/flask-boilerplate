How does it work

I

In collaboration with farmers and horticultural engineers, Vaerens grows crops indoors under strictly controlled conditions

II

Imaging and sensory data is harvested from the crops; deep learning analysis is then applied

III

Iterations of data farming cycles are optimised by an artificial intelligence driven tuning strategy

IV

The harvested data is used to train artificial intelligence models to make predictions about optimised growth conditions
