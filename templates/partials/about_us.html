About Vaerens



Vaerens is a Phyto(plant-)technology startup company based in the Netherlands. Our mission is to have an impact on the relationship that exists between humans and the ecosystems that support human life, with plants center-stage in how we position our ambitions. Plants have played a pivotal role in the genesis of our species and we believe that they will play the same role in overcoming the threats that have evolved to our continued existence on this planet. The flexibility of the genetic material of plants, combined with extraordinary willingness of plant organisms to be cloned, bred and conditioned, mean that also in the future, plants are going to be there for us; supporting our continued survival on a healthy earth.
{# And supporting our species as we venture out into the solar system and beyond.#}

The evolution of such technologies though, has been hampered by a lack of quantitative understanding of the relationship between plant genetic material and grow conditions. In 2020 Mit Koevoets started Vaerens with the purpose of building a company on the pillars of plant science, data science, horticulture- and software engineering.
