Mit Koevoets

Founder & CEO


Born in Rotterdam; Mit Koevoets grew up in the Betuwe, the heart of outdoor fruit cultivation in the Netherlands; where growing food and horticulture practices are part of the fabric of every day life. At the Royal Academy of Arts in The Hague, he studied programming, electronics and 3d modelling; collaborating with the TU Delft on environmental modelling and working on various projects developing sound recognition and self-learning genetic algorithms over the years at the academy. After getting his BA, he spent a year working as a freelance 3d modeller in the 3d printing industry and then moved to Italy for 2,5 years to study classical arts. The following 7 years he built a career as an established software engineer, focusing on web technologies initially and then moving on to machine learning engineering, working on a contract for Apple in 2018 and ING in 2019. In 2020 Mit founded Vaerens with an ambition to develop sustainable cultivation methods for agriculture driven by data and plant science.

Mattia Houtenbrink

Horticultural Engineer


Mattia Houtenbrink was born in the port city Rotterdam, neighboring the famous Westland, the cradle of Dutch horticulture, to the direct west. Over the past 7 years, Mattia has worked passionately to learn everything he can about growing cannabis in controlled environments. After being rejected from Horticural College for being to open about his desire to have a career in the legal cannabis greenhosue industry, he followed an internship at Wageningen University to learn more about greenhouse growing. Mattia started 010Genetics in 2019 with the intention to promote his cannabis genetics library. He joined Vaerens in early 2020 as a horticultural engineer driven by his ambition to be part of a company where he can both apply his expertise in cannabis cultivation and have an opportunitiy to work with other crops besides cannabis. He brings on board among others his excellent insights into cultivar breeding, facility design and plant training.
